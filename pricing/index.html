Pricing FAQ

What's included in each record?

Every record includes practice name, address, business phone, website URL, owner or decision-maker name, NPI number, taxonomy codes, and LinkedIn profile. Growth plans add practice firmographics and technology detection.

Can I get a sample before purchasing?

Yes. We provide a free sample of 25-50 records matched to your target criteria so you can validate data quality before committing to a purchase.

Do you offer email or mobile phone data?

Yes. Direct email and mobile phone enrichment are available as add-ons. Contact us for enrichment pricing based on your volume and match rate requirements.

How fast can I get my data?

Most orders ship within a few business days. We routinely turn around 10,000+ records in a single day for standard specialties and geographies. Custom requests with unusual filtering criteria may take longer.

What if I need a specialty or geography you don't cover?

Contact us. We can build custom datasets for specialties or regions outside our standard coverage.
