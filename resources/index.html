Healthcare Provider Data Guides, Articles, and Resources

Practical guides on sourcing, verifying, and using healthcare provider business data. Written for the sales, marketing, and operations teams that depend on accurate provider intelligence.

Learn About Healthcare Provider Data

Healthcare provider data is a specialized category of B2B intelligence. It includes NPI numbers, practice addresses, direct phone lines, verified email addresses, specialty taxonomy codes, practice size indicators, and technology signals. Understanding how this data is sourced, verified, and maintained is the difference between campaigns that convert and outreach that bounces.

We write these guides for the people who actually use provider data day to day: sales development reps building call lists, marketing managers running email campaigns, operations teams maintaining CRM hygiene, and analysts sizing new markets. Each article focuses on a specific topic and gives you actionable guidance rather than generic overviews. You will find sourced statistics, real-world examples, and specific recommendations tied to healthcare provider workflows.

Topics range from foundational concepts like how the CMS NPI Registry works and what NUCC taxonomy codes mean, to applied strategies like building account-based marketing programs for healthcare and calculating the ROI of data quality investments. We update these guides as the provider data landscape changes, including new data sources, evolving best practices, and shifts in how healthcare organizations manage their provider information.

Whether you are new to healthcare provider data or optimizing an existing data strategy, these resources are designed to help you make better decisions about how you source, verify, and deploy provider intelligence across your organization.
