Frequently Asked Questions

What physical therapy provider data does Provyx offer?

We provide verified practice data and owner contacts for 7 types of physical therapy providers, including practice details, NPI numbers, taxonomy codes, website, and LinkedIn profile. All records are verified against the CMS NPI Registry. Direct email and mobile enrichment available as add-ons.

How is physical therapy data different from generic provider databases?

Generic databases lump all healthcare providers together without understanding physical therapy specialty distinctions. We segment by specific provider subtypes, making it easy to target exactly the right physical therapy providers for your campaign.

How often is the physical therapy provider data updated?

Our physical therapy provider database is continuously verified against multiple sources. Provider contacts change frequently as staff turns over and practices relocate. Our verification process catches these changes to keep your outreach data current.

Can I get a custom physical therapy provider list?

Yes. We build custom lists filtered by specific physical therapy subtypes, geography, practice size, and other criteria. Contact us with your requirements and we'll put together a matched list.

What data fields are included for physical therapy providers?

Every record includes practice name, address, business phone, website URL, owner or decision-maker name, NPI number, taxonomy codes, and LinkedIn profile. Growth plans add practice firmographics and technology detection. Direct email and mobile enrichment available as add-ons.
