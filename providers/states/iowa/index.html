Regulatory Environment

The Iowa Board of Medicine oversees physician licensing with a streamlined process. Iowa was an early adopter of telehealth-friendly regulations and grants full practice authority to nurse practitioners, helping serve rural populations.

Market Overview

University of Iowa Hospitals and Clinics in Iowa City is the state's flagship academic medical center and a major referral destination. UnityPoint Health and MercyOne are the largest multi-hospital systems. Iowa's network of 82 critical access hospitals is one of the densest in the nation.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many healthcare providers are in Iowa?

Iowa has over 28,000 active healthcare providers, including approximately 7,800 physicians, 1,900 dental practices, and 3,200 mental health professionals.

What role do rural hospitals play in Iowa?

Iowa has 82 critical access hospitals, among the most of any state. These small facilities are essential to rural communities, providing emergency care, primary care, and basic inpatient services.

Is Iowa facing provider shortages?

Yes, particularly in rural western and southern Iowa. Primary care and mental health providers are in highest demand. The state offers loan repayment and residency programs to encourage rural practice.
