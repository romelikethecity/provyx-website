Frequently Asked Questions

What neurology provider data does Provyx offer?

We provide verified contact data for 2 types of neurology providers, including direct emails, phone numbers, NPI details, taxonomy codes, and practice information. All records are verified against the CMS NPI Registry.

How is neurology data different from generic provider databases?

Generic databases lump all healthcare providers together without understanding neurology specialty distinctions. We segment by specific provider subtypes, making it easy to target exactly the right neurology providers for your campaign.

How often is the neurology provider data updated?

Our neurology provider database is refreshed weekly. Provider contacts change frequently as staff turns over and practices relocate. Weekly updates keep your outreach data current.

Can I get a custom neurology provider list?

Yes. We build custom lists filtered by specific neurology subtypes, geography, practice size, and other criteria. Contact us with your requirements and we'll put together a matched list.

What data fields are included for neurology providers?

Every record includes provider name, NPI number, taxonomy code, practice name, address, and available contact information (email, phone). Additional fields like practice size, technology stack, and ownership type are available on Growth and Enterprise plans.
