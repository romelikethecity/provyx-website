Dental Provider Data & Contact Lists

Comprehensive dental provider data including general dentists, specialists, and dental labs with verified contacts.

Dental practices are among the most fragmented in healthcare. There are over 200,000 practicing dentists in the US, spread across solo practices, group practices, and DSOs. Finding the right contacts with accurate, current information is the difference between a successful campaign and wasted spend. Our dental provider database covers general dentists and every major specialty.

What is Dental Provider Data?

Dental Provider Data is verified contact and practice intelligence for dental providers, including practice details, NPI numbers, taxonomy codes, owner contacts, and technology data, segmented by 16 specific provider subtypes.

Provyx segments dental providers by subtype so you can target precisely the right practices for your campaign, rather than working with a generic list that lumps all dental providers together.

Frequently Asked Questions

What dental provider data does Provyx offer?

We provide verified practice data and owner contacts for 16 types of dental providers, including practice details, NPI numbers, taxonomy codes, website, and LinkedIn profile. All records are verified against the CMS NPI Registry. Direct email and mobile enrichment available as add-ons.

How is dental data different from generic provider databases?

Generic databases lump all healthcare providers together without understanding dental specialty distinctions. We segment by specific provider subtypes, making it easy to target exactly the right dental providers for your campaign.

How often is the dental provider data updated?

Our dental provider database is continuously verified against multiple sources. Provider contacts change frequently as staff turns over and practices relocate. Our verification process catches these changes to keep your outreach data current.

Can I get a custom dental provider list?

Yes. We build custom lists filtered by specific dental subtypes, geography, practice size, and other criteria. Contact us with your requirements and we'll put together a matched list.

What data fields are included for dental providers?

Every record includes practice name, address, business phone, website URL, owner or decision-maker name, NPI number, taxonomy codes, and LinkedIn profile. Growth plans add practice firmographics and technology detection. Direct email and mobile enrichment available as add-ons.
