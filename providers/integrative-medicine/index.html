Frequently Asked Questions

What integrative medicine provider data does Provyx offer?

We provide verified contact data for 12 types of integrative medicine providers, including direct emails, phone numbers, NPI details, taxonomy codes, and practice information. All records are verified against the CMS NPI Registry.

How is integrative medicine data different from generic provider databases?

Generic databases lump all healthcare providers together without understanding integrative medicine specialty distinctions. We segment by specific provider subtypes, making it easy to target exactly the right integrative medicine providers for your campaign.

How often is the integrative medicine provider data updated?

Our integrative medicine provider database is continuously verified against multiple sources. Provider contacts change frequently as staff turns over and practices relocate. Our verification process catches these changes to keep your outreach data current.

Can I get a custom integrative medicine provider list?

Yes. We build custom lists filtered by specific integrative medicine subtypes, geography, practice size, and other criteria. Contact us with your requirements and we'll put together a matched list.

What data fields are included for integrative medicine providers?

Every record includes provider name, NPI number, taxonomy code, practice name, address, and available contact information (email, phone). Additional fields like practice size, technology stack, and ownership type are available on Growth and Enterprise plans.
